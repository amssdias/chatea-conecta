Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/05/2025

Welcome to Chatea-Conecta. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information collected from users (“you” or “your”) while using our application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information. The only information we process is the username you create upon accessing our app. This username can be any set of characters of your choice and is not linked to your personal identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

The username is used exclusively to facilitate the chat functionality within the app. We do not store usernames permanently; they are temporarily cached using a secure caching service (Redis) and are automatically removed when you leave the page.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. A single cookie is used to store the username you create for the duration of your session. This cookie is deleted once the session ends or the page is refreshed.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Usernames are stored temporarily in Redis, a secure caching service, and are automatically deleted when the user exits the page.
  • We implement reasonable technical measures to ensure that the temporary data processed by our app is secure.

5. Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or disclose your information to third parties.

6. Age Restrictions

Our app is intended for users aged 18 and older. By using this app, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. If you are under 18, you are solely responsible for ensuring compliance with this restriction.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not store or collect personal information, there are no specific rights for data access, correction, or deletion. If you have concerns about privacy, you may stop using the app at any time.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and the updated date will be indicated at the top.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please refrain from using the app.
